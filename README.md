# Power-BI-Dashboard---Bitter-to-Better-chocolate-profit-insights-
This Power BI dashboard analyzes a chocolate business by tracking revenue, profit, cost, and sales across products, regions, and teams. It highlights top-performing products and identifies gaps between sales and profitability. Overall, it delivers clear insights to improve business performance.

## Overview

This project analyzes chocolate business performance across products, regions, and sales teams using a Power BI dashboard. It converts raw sales data into actionable insights to track revenue, profit, and overall business efficiency.

## Objective

To evaluate sales performance, identify high-performing products and regions, analyze profitability, and support data-driven business decisions.

## Key Insights
Certain products generate high revenue but lower profit margins
A few regions contribute the majority of total sales
Sales performance varies significantly across categories
Monthly trends highlight fluctuations in demand and revenue
Profitability is driven more by margin than just sales volume
## Features
KPI tracking (Revenue, Profit, Sales Volume)
Product and category performance analysis
Regional sales comparison
Monthly trend analysis
Profit vs sales insights
Interactive filtering for deeper analysis
